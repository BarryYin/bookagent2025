解说词：
We're excited to introduce a new perspective, a guide to navigating the complexities of life with grace and resilience. Based on the wisdom of a 90-year-old psychologist, Dr. Tsuneko Nakamura, '人间值得' – or 'Life is Worth Living' – offers a profound understanding of what truly matters. We believe this book will resonate deeply with anyone seeking peace and purpose.
